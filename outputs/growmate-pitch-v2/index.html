Social Good

Making a Real Difference

GrowMate aligns with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals — because responsible plant trading changes communities and ecosystems.

SDG 11 — Sustainable Cities

Safer Urban Greening

GrowMate supports urban gardening and food-plant adoption, helping city dwellers build plant-filled, resilient communities through accessible local plant trade.

SDG 12 — Responsible Consumption

Ethical Plant Trade

Leafy AI helps buyers make informed choices. Sellers get clear plant data and safety checks so the plant trade stays legal, ethical, and sustainable for everyone.

Protecting Biodiversity

Automatic detection of rare or endangered species prevents illegal wildlife plant trade and actively protects Philippine biodiversity at the community level.
