Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2026

Important Data Security Note: GrowMate uses industry-standard encryption protocols on mobile devices to preserve the privacy of our gardeners, ensuring credentials and communications remain safe.

At GrowMate (operated under plantita.online), we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and share your personal information when you use our mobile application and our website services.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with a functional, safe, and highly personalized experience, we collect several categories of information:

  • Account Information: When you register on GrowMate, your authentication details (such as email address, name, and securely hashed passwords) are handled directly and securely via Supabase Authentication services. We do not store raw, unencrypted passwords on our servers.
  • Profile & Garden Information: Information you voluntarily share on your public profile, such as your garden name, location, short biography, avatar picture, and cover photos.
  • Marketplace & Care Data: Information about the plants you list for sale (photos, descriptions, prices), transaction history, plant health tracker logs, care schedules, and community forum posts.
  • Device Storage & Encryption: On mobile clients, we use the device's native secure keychains (such as Expo SecureStore on iOS and Android) to encrypt and save your session tokens locally. This ensures your account remains logged in securely without exposing your credentials.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following core purposes:

  • Service Operation: Managing your account, enabling peer-to-peer chat, displaying localized plant listings, and processing orders.
  • Leafy AI Assistance: When you upload a photo to identify a plant or create a listing, the photo is processed by Leafy AI to generate suggestions. The images are used solely for classification purposes.
  • Security & Compliance: Monitoring and reviewing marketplace listings to ensure safety, prevent fraudulent behavior, and avoid the illegal trade of protected or endangered wild plants.
  • Personalization: Displaying relative plant collections and recommending local horticultural groups in your province.

3. Data Storage & Sharing

Your data is stored in secure databases operated by our infrastructure providers. We do not sell or lease your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. Your information is only shared under the following conditions:

  • Peer-to-Peer Interaction: When you list a plant or participate in community discussions, your public garden profile, rankings, and active listings are visible to other users.
  • Service Providers: We use Supabase as our secure database and hosting provider. Their services comply with strict data security standards.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose information if required to do so by Philippine laws or in response to valid legal requests by government authorities.

4. Your Rights and Choices

We believe you should have control over your data. Under the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173) of the Philippines, you are entitled to the following rights:

  • Access and Correction: You can view and edit your profile information and active listings at any time directly in the app.
  • Account Deletion: You have the right to request the deletion of your account and all associated personal data. To request account deletion, please email us directly at support@plantita.online. We will process your request within 5-10 business days.

5. Security of Your Data

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. All network communication between the app and our database is encrypted over SSL/TLS. Account tokens are stored using SecureStore hardware encryption on-device, preventing unauthorized access even if the device is compromised.

6. Changes to this Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date above.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact our team at:

Email: support@plantita.online
