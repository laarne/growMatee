Support Categories & Email Guidelines

🛡️ Buyer Disputes

If a seller does not arrive for a meetup or the plant delivered does not match the listing, please email us with your Order ID, description of the issue, and photos of the plant received. We will freeze the transaction and investigate.

💼 Seller Concerns

For questions regarding platform fees (10%), listing flags, listing approval, or issues with buyers not completing handoff confirmations, please write to us with the listing or transaction details.

🗑️ Account Deletion

To delete your GrowMate profile and remove your data from our database in compliance with the Data Privacy Act, please email us using your registered email address with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".

🤝 Partnerships

Are you a plant organization, local horticultural club, or conservation group in the Philippines? We'd love to collaborate on sustainable communities. Get in touch via our support email.
