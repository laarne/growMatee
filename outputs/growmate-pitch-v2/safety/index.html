Marketplace Safety Rules

Last updated: May 30, 2026

Community Priority: GrowMate was built to create a safe, sustainable ecosystem for local gardeners across the Philippines. Following these rules protects you, your plants, and other gardeners.

1. How Orders Work

All listings and transactions on the GrowMate app follow a structured pipeline designed to prevent confusion and fraud:

  1. Order Request: The buyer clicks "Request Order" on a listing and inputs their preferred meetup location or delivery instructions. The listing is temporarily reserved.
  2. Seller Review: The seller reviews the request and discusses delivery details with the buyer via the secure in-app chat. The seller can then accept or reject the order.
  3. Acceptance & Scheduling: Once accepted, the meetup or local courier delivery (such as Lalamove, Grab, or Borzo) is scheduled.
  4. Physical Inspection & Handoff: When the plant changes hands, the buyer inspects it.
  5. Completion: Once satisfied, the buyer clicks "Confirm Receipt" in the app. The transaction is marked complete, and any platform fee is processed.

2. Cancellation Policy

We understand that schedules and plant availability can change. Our cancellation rules are as follows:

  • Before Dispatch/Meetup: Both buyers and sellers have the right to cancel an order request at any time prior to the seller marking the order as "dispatched" or "on the way."
  • Seller Cancellations: If a seller frequently cancels orders after accepting them due to lack of stock or incorrect care, their seller ranking may be lowered, and their visibility on the marketplace feed reduced.
  • Buyer Cancellations: Buyers who repeatedly request orders and cancel them without valid reasons will be flagged, and their account may face restrictions on the maximum number of active order requests allowed.

3. Damaged Plant Policy

Plants are fragile living specimens. Because of their nature, special rules apply to damage claims:

  • Inspection is Mandatory: The buyer must inspect the plant's leaves, stems, and soil condition immediately during the meetup or upon delivery handoff.
  • Right to Reject: If the plant is damaged, heavily pest-infested, or looks significantly different from the listing photos, the buyer has the right to reject the handoff. The buyer should not confirm receipt in the app and should cancel the order indicating "Item not as described".
  • After Confirmation: Once the buyer clicks "Confirm Receipt" inside the app, the transaction is closed. No refunds, returns, or claims will be processed by GrowMate after this step.

4. Dispute Resolution Process

If an issue arises during a transaction (e.g. a seller fails to meet up or ships the wrong plant, or a buyer claims a plant was not delivered):

  1. Contact Support: Do not confirm receipt in the app. Email our dispute team immediately at support@plantita.online.
  2. Provide Evidence: Include the Order ID, seller/buyer name, screenshot of chat history, and clear photographs of the plant (if delivered).
  3. Investigation: Our team will review the in-app chat transcripts and delivery documentation. We will make a final decision on order cancellation or fund release within 3-5 business days.

5. Rules Against Outside-App Transactions

To ensure a safe environment, we strictly prohibit conducting transactions outside of the GrowMate app if they were initiated on the platform.

Warning: Direct transactions (e.g. paying outside the app via personal bank transfers before handoff, or completing cash deals without using the app's order tracker) bypass our safety checks. We cannot provide dispute assistance, refund support, or verify user credibility for transactions that occur off-platform. Violators will face immediate account suspension.

6. What "Buyer Protection" Actually Means

When you transact inside the GrowMate order pipeline, you are covered by our Buyer Protection Policy. This means:

  • Payment Escrow: Your funds are held securely by the platform and are only released to the seller after you confirm receipt.
  • Resolution Assistance: If the seller does not ship or bring the plant, you get a full refund.
  • Quality Hold: If the plant arrives dead or is the wrong species, you can freeze the transaction by opening a dispute before confirming receipt.
